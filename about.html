What this is

Integros Flashcards is your personal drill engine for exams, infra concepts, CHAMP, AZ-900, or anything else you care enough to encode.

Decks live in JSON. The browser stores your progress locally, no account, no sync, no noise.

How to use it

  • Go to Deck Explorer to import or tweak decks.
  • Head to Study and choose a mode: normal, multiple choice, type answer, or spaced order.
  • Repeat until the cards feel boring. Then you know it is working.
