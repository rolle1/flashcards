What this is

Integros Flashcards is your personal drill engine for exams or anything else you care enough to encode.

Decks live in JSON. The browser stores your progress locally. Optionally sync across devices with Google Drive.

How to use it

  • Go to Explore to import or tweak decks.
  • Head to Study and choose a mode: normal or multiple choice.
  • Repeat until the cards feel boring. Then you know it is working.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Space — flip the card
  • ← → — previous / next card
  • F — flag the current card for review
